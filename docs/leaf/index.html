Louisiana Economic Activity Forecast Reports

I began producing these quarterly reports in the spring of 2020. Forecasts are generated for (statewide) employment, unemployment rate, home prices, and GDP. Employment forecasts are also provided separately for each of the state’s nine metropolitan statistical areas.
